Established in 2009, Team RoboManipal is the esteemed official robotics team of MAHE, Manipal, comprising a multifaceted ensemble of over forty fervent undergraduate students united by their enthusiasm for robotics.

Our 14-year journey marked by accomplishments through prestigious competitions like ABU ROBOCON, our flagship competition. We also organize Robowars for TechTatva, MAHE's technical fest. Additionally, we're researching projects like a 6-degree-of-freedom robot, a humanoid biped, and a quadruped robot, which showcases our unwavering commitment.

| OUR GOALS AND VISION |

We harbor grand visions for the future of robotics, envisioning it as a catalyst propelling humanity forward through groundbreaking technologies. Our belief lies in robotics' ability to nurture creativity, providing a fertile ground for innovative ideas to flourish. Undeterred, we embrace the most demanding global robotics competitions.

Driven by a thirst for knowledge, we devise ingenious solutions to complex problems, leveraging robotics' vast potential and applications. Our commitment lies in unlocking this ever-evolving domain's full potential, shaping a future where robotics drives human progress.
